Travels

A quick map of my travels. I was born and raised in Aledo, TX, a humble but growing city. After graduating highschool, I chose to attend Texas A&M University, located in College Station, TX, and pursue a Bachelors' Degree in Computer Science. Since enrolling, I have been fortunate enough to work for two great companies as an intern/co-op. I spent eight months over the summer and fall of 2016 in Greenville, TX working as a software engineer co-op for L-3 Technologies, where I was exposed to the defense industry and the different technologies coming to market for the world's militaries. Thereafter, I spent time in San Jose, CA as a software engineer summer intern for Cisco Systems, contributing a small piece towards a massive effort to enable wireless networks to detect malware in encrypted network traffic.
